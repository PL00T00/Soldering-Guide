<h1>Getting started!</h1>

Soldering can be the easiest, most meditative thing ever but it can also be stressful and hard. This guide is made for complete beginners who have never held a soldering iron before but hopefully can even help out the more intermediate folks.

<h2>What you'll need</h2>
<h3>Required</h3>

- A soldering iron

- solder (I reccomend a 60/40 leaded blend with flux)
- Something to solder
- A good area to solder (I use an old tile I put on an old table but a silicone mat on your desk also works!)
- Safety gear (Soldering isn't inherintly dangerous but in the rare case something goes wrong it's better to be safe than sorry.)
- A damp sponge
<h3>Optional (but good to have if you want to solder frequently)</h3>

- Extra flux

- Solder sucker
- Helping hands / PCB clamp
- Flux-off

<h2>Safety</h2>

Soldering is normally extremely safe but anything could go wrong so make sure your being extremely safe around your iron.
